About Me

Werner Hansen | Tito

Currently, I'm focused on advancing my career as a User Experience Designer, and the Advanced
Diploma program at Georgian College in Toronto plays a crucial role in enhancing my knowledge
of human-computer interaction. I'm passionate about designing with purpose, weaving human
elements into interfaces, and refining interactions to flow as naturally as everyday gestures.


Background

For as long as I can remember, I've been curious and fascinated by how people perceive and interact
with the world around them. This curiosity led me to study psychology and cognitive science, delving
into the intricacies of human perception, cognition, and ultimately, human-computer interaction.

Design Approach

My journey has shaped my design approach to prioritize reducing users' cognitive load. In a world
where we deal with numerous products daily, the last thing I want is to add another obstacle. Instead,
I aim to create inviting experiences that encourage users to explore more deeply without feeling
disoriented from the start.

One aspect of design that captivates me is how small details can subtly influence people's experiences.
It's not just about aesthetics; it's about achieving that perfect balance where everything fits seamlessly.
That's why I enjoy creating playful micro-animations, refining animations to achieve ideal fluidity,
and humanizing interactions to reflect genuine human intentions. Consider this: often, it's the little
things that brighten your day!

At the core of my work is empathy. Remembering that there's a complex and unique individual on the other
side of the screen inspires me to adopt a multi-layered design approach. My goal is to create products that
bring satisfaction to people and seamlessly integrate into their daily lives.

Passions

I find inspiration in the world around me. I love exploring new cultures through travel, which often influences
my design decisions. When I'm not creating, you can find me cycling, playing tennis, or playing with my dog
Shamira. I also have a special appreciation for pizza, but then again, who doesn't?" :)
